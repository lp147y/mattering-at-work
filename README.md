# Mattering at Work

A 6-week wellbeing campaign designed for veterinary healthcare teams, 
exploring the concept of mattering — feeling valued, noticed, and needed 
at work.

## About

This campaign was developed by Luiz Santos (DVM, MSc, PhD, Dip.ACVAA) 
at the University of Glasgow's Small Animal Hospital.

Each week explores a different dimension of mattering in the workplace, 
drawing on psychological safety, human factors, and staff wellbeing research.
